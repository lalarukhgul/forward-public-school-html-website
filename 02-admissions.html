Forward Public School, Hayatabad

Admissions Open

Forward Public School welcomes students who are eager to learn and grow in a positive educational environment.

Admission Procedure

  1. Obtain the admission form.
  2. Fill out the application form completely.
  3. Submit required documents.
  4. Appear for an entrance assessment (if applicable).
  5. Attend an interview with parents and student.
  6. Receive admission confirmation.
  7. Complete fee payment.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • B-Form/NADRA Child Registration Certificate
  • Previous School Result Card
  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Recent Passport Size Photographs
  • Parent/Guardian CNIC Copy

Eligibility Criteria

Class Age Requirement
Nursery 3-4 Years
KG 4-5 Years
Grade 1 5-6 Years
Grade 2-10 According to previous class completion

Admission Benefits

  • Quality education
  • Qualified teachers
  • Modern learning facilities
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Safe learning environment
